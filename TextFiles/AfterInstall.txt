Thanks for installing Rigs of Rods!

By default, Rigs of Rods only comes with a very small selection of vehicles and terrains. 
For the best experience download some mods from the Repository: https://forum.rigsofrods.org/downloads.php 
The Showroom Subforum (https://forum.rigsofrods.org/content/) and the Archives (http://archives.rigsofrods.net/) contain additional content not found in the Repository.
